How Can Maxum Help With Your Petroleum Needs?

You won't have to look very hard to find that Maxum is the leader in oil blending and fuel and lubricant delivery

Lubricant Blending

Maxum is the #1 manufacturer of commercial and industrial, automotive, and marine lubricants on the West coast. We also provide contract manufacturing services, terminaling, and other related services.

Marine Products

Maxum supplies and delivers millions of gallons of Marine fuel and lubricant each year to major ports all across the Western United States.

Commercial Products

Maxum is the largest provider of commercial grade fuel and lubricant products in the Western United States, supplying more than 10 millions gallons of blended lubricant alone in 2013.

What makes Maxum the right choice

  • The Right Mix

    Maxum's own in-house laboratory undergoes rigorous and extensive compliancy testing to ensure our products are the highest quality available.

  • Dedicated Staff

    Maxum has a proven track record of low employee turn over. We're a company of employees you'll know and can depend on. We believe an experienced staff translates to satisfied customers.

  • Precision

    Maxum is the only petroleum blending company utilizing the latest Foxboro precision blending automation technology.

  • Competative Rates

    Our sales and business development team work rigorously to purchase base oils and fuel at the lowest possible market rate - and we're happy to pass the savings on to you.

  • Coverage

    Maxum is the largest petroleum distribution company in the Western United States, with dock and port service in Seattle, Portland, San Francisco, and the Port of LA/Long Beach.

  • Partnership

    We're confident in our products, our team, and our service. Maxum sees itself as a partner with our customers, not just a provider.
