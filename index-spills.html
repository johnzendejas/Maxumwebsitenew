Spill Response Information

This website is your direct communication to Maxum Petroleum in response to a possible oil spill or environmental release or concern.

Maxum is dedicated to ensuring that personal safety and environmental concerns are our top priorities.

Claims may be based on (but not limited to) the following:

  • Individual Economic Loss
  • Business Economic Loss
  • Vessel Physical Damage
  • Start-up Business Economic Loss
  • Failed Business Economic Loss
  • Coastal Real Property Damage
  • Real Property Sales Loss
